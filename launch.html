Tenera QuickBooks Internal Reporting Tool

This private internal tool is used by authorized Tenera Care personnel to refresh QuickBooks Online Profit and Loss and Balance Sheet reports into a Tenera Care-controlled Google Sheet.

Purpose

The application retrieves accounting report data from Tenera Care's own QuickBooks Online company account using Intuit's API. The data is used for internal finance, management reporting, planning, and analysis.

Authorized access

This tool is not available to customers, vendors, or the public. Authorized users should access the reporting spreadsheet through Tenera Care's Google Workspace.
