Tenera QuickBooks Internal Reporting Tool - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Overview

This internal application is used only by Tenera Care to retrieve Tenera Care's own QuickBooks Online accounting reports through Intuit's API.

Data accessed

The application may access QuickBooks Online accounting report data, including Profit and Loss, Balance Sheet, account names, balances, and related financial reporting information.

Purpose of use

The data is used only for internal finance, management reporting, planning, and analysis.

Data storage

Report data may be written to Tenera Care-controlled Google Sheets within Tenera Care's Google Workspace environment. OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored in Google Apps Script user properties associated with the authorized Tenera Care user.

Data sharing

The application is not offered to third parties and does not sell or share data with advertisers or unrelated third parties. Data may be available to authorized Tenera Care personnel and service providers used by Tenera Care to operate Google Workspace, QuickBooks Online, and related internal systems.

Security

Access is restricted to authorized Tenera Care users. The application uses OAuth authorization to connect to QuickBooks Online.

Contact

Questions about this internal application may be directed to stephen@tenera.care.
