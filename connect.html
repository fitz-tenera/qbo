Connect or Reconnect QuickBooks

Authorized Tenera Care users can connect this internal reporting tool to QuickBooks Online from the controlled Google Sheet.

Connection steps

  1. Open the Tenera Care QuickBooks reporting spreadsheet in Google Sheets.
  2. Select QuickBooks → Authorize QuickBooks.
  3. Sign in with an Intuit account that has access to Tenera Care's QuickBooks Online company.
  4. Approve the requested access.
  5. Return to the Google Sheet and use the QuickBooks menu to refresh reports.

Reconnect steps

  1. Open the reporting spreadsheet.
  2. Select QuickBooks → Reset QuickBooks Auth.
  3. Select QuickBooks → Authorize QuickBooks.
  4. Sign in with an authorized Intuit account and approve access.
