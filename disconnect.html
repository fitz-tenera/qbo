QuickBooks Connection Disconnected

The connection between Tenera Care's internal reporting tool and QuickBooks Online has been disconnected.

Effect of disconnecting

The reporting spreadsheet will no longer be able to refresh QuickBooks Online reports until an authorized user reconnects the application.

Reconnect steps

  1. Open the Tenera Care QuickBooks reporting spreadsheet in Google Sheets.
  2. Select QuickBooks → Reset QuickBooks Auth.
  3. Select QuickBooks → Authorize QuickBooks.
  4. Sign in with an Intuit account that has access to Tenera Care's QuickBooks Online company.
  5. Approve the requested access.
