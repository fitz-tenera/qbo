Tenera QuickBooks Internal Reporting Tool

This is a private internal reporting tool used only by authorized Tenera Care personnel.

The tool connects to Tenera Care's own QuickBooks Online company account using Intuit's API and retrieves accounting reports for internal finance and management reporting.
